Instructions:

Rock: a closed fist.
Paper: all fingers extended, palm facing down.
Scissors: index and middle fingers extended and separated forming a "V".
The objective is to defeat the opponent by selecting the weapon that wins, according to the following rules:

Results

  • The rock crushes the scissors. (The rock wins.)
  • The scissors cut the paper. (Scissors wins.)
  • The paper wraps the stone. (Paper Wins.)
